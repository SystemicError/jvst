Score Guide

How to interpret your vocabulary size score:

This Japanese vocabulary size test contains 100 randomly selected words from a large corpus of novels, magazines, internet blogs, and other written materials. Your score is a rough approximation of the number of words you know in Japanese*.
*If you score higher than 9,000, this vocabulary size test may not have enough advanced items to accurately estimate your score.

How to interpret your vocabulary profile:

Your vocabulary profile approximates the percent of words you know for each frequency band from the 1-1,000 band to the 9,001-10,000 band. The words within the 1,000 band represent some of the most frequent words used in written Japanese texts, while words in the 10,000 band level are much less frequent. Your vocabulary profile provides a rough estimate of the percentage of words you know in each frequency band.
